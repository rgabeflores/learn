About

As a Computer Science student, I was interested in developing an expertise in a single programming language. I chose Python because of its broad applicability and relevance in modern technology. Since its creation, this blog has become a resource for teaching others about the invaluable programming concepts that I had been learning myself.

There are plenty of resources online to learn how to code in Python. However, it is difficult to find advanced topics. After reaching a plateau in my personal studies, I decided to dig deeper. These posts on Instagram are a result of what I have discovered and understood (so far).
