About

I (rgabeflores.github.io) created @learn.py to simultaneously learn advanced Python programming and introduce Computer Science theory to the social media community. I decided to document images of my personal code and understandings on Instagram, under the slightest possibility that at least one other aspiring STEM student may find value. I chose Instagram because it is often considered to be the easiest mobile app to use on a daily basis, and in which anyone scrolling through their timeline can effortlessly come across something new and valuable.
